Home page

Goal of Website

The goal of the website is to provide a professional portfolio of my engagement and wedding photography, give people information about my pricing, and allow people to get in contact with me. All these things are there to convince my target audience to purchase my photography services.

Porfolio Goal

Probably the most important aspect of the website is providing a portfolio of my work. Ideally I could have one portfolio pages with multiple smaller portfolios of different shoots you can access from there. Everyone likes looking at nice pictures, but I really need to use the best images possible to get a wow reaction.

Bio Goal

Making a connection with my clients is super important, I mean no one really cares if you took nice photos if you were a giant pain to work with, you couldn’t get your clients to pose properly, and they do not like how they looked in any of the photos. That is why my about me is so crucial to make a connection with my clients and seem human.

Why choose Lawson Photography?

With 5 years of professional photography experience, and two years of shooting weddings, you can count on me to provide the best possible images with a seamless enjoyable shooting experience. My goal is to create timeless art that you can enjoy and treasure for the rest of your life. I do that through shooting with the most advanced photography equipment available, constantly learning, and constantly improving my skills as a photographer. No matter how nice a photograph is, if you don’t like the way you look it’s not a good picture! I take a lot of time to understand how to pose people, give clear instructions on posing, and have as much fun as possible to capture natural meaningful interactions. I hope that you enjoy my portfolio because I take a lot of pride in my work.

About me

My name is Lawson Turcotte. I am originally from Rexburg Idaho but am currently living in Logan. Photography has been a hobby of mine since I was a teenager, and have shot professionally for 5 years, and have two years of wedding experience. When I’m not taking pictures some other hobbies that I enjoy are cycling, backpacking, snowboarding, skateboarding, and reading. I come from a family of 6 kids, and love to spend my time with them! I also have an absolutely incredible fiancee named Ellie.

Pricing Goal

I want to keep my pricing as simple to understand as possible, and keep it competitively priced. A lot of photographers have kind of complicated pricing plans so I want to avoid that as much as possible.

Wedding packages -

Wedding package - $2200

This includes up to 6 hours of shooting and 400 edited photos. Each additional hour of shooting will be an additional $150.

Engagement package - $250

This includes 2 hours of shooting and 100 edited photos

Senior photos and one on one portraits - $150

This includes one hour of shooting and 50 edited photos

Event photography - $3000

Includes all day coverage of an event and 500 edited photos
